Frequently asked questions

How does matching work?

Our AI analyses your message, urgency and category, then surfaces experts ranked by fit, rating and availability.

How are experts verified?

Each expert submits credentials and ID which our team reviews manually before activation.

What payment methods are supported?

Razorpay (UPI, cards, netbanking) and Stripe (cards, wallets).

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes — cancel anytime from your dashboard. Your plan stays active until the billing period ends.
